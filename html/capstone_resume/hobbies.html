Hobbies

Japanese Light Novels

These books are usually 300 pages long and are about another world from ours. The theme is often video-game related and skill building. It's very fluffy, but I love escaping to a safe place and able to predict the storyline.

Playing with My Dog - Momo

My dog is 8 years-old and is an American Eskimo. She is a sweetheart and loves chasing after balls. I love her personality. She makes my day better and I cannot imagine the time I will have to say goodbye to such a good girl.

Dungeons and Dragon Podcast

The last few years, I have been able to play Dungeons and Dragons only a handful of times. So the way I can get around this is by listening to the games that other people run. Now, I am enjoying a very full story of Critical Role's 3rd Campaign.
